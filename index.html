About Kenric UG

Your Trusted Travel Partner in Uganda

Founded in 2015, Kenric UG Travel Company has grown to become one of the most reliable ridesharing services in Uganda. What started as a small fleet of three vehicles has now expanded to over 50 cars serving customers across major cities and towns.

Our mission is to provide safe, comfortable, and affordable transportation solutions while contributing to the reduction of traffic congestion and carbon emissions through ridesharing.

All our drivers undergo rigorous training and background checks to ensure your safety and comfort. We maintain our vehicles to the highest standards to provide you with a smooth and enjoyable ride every time.
